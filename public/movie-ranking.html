The Best Movies According to Aika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Naruto Shippuden

This is my favourite anime because Naruto Shippuden explores powerful themes of friendship, loyalty, and perseverance. It emphasizes the value of genuine connections and highlights the strength that comes from unity and mutual support. These themes resonate with viewers and contribute to the series' enduring popularity.

2. Detective Conan

Conan investigates the murder of a famous conductor at the Beethoven Symphony Hall. As he uncovers a connection to a mysterious musical composition, he must solve the case before the killer strikes again. Thrilling Detective Conan adventure combines music and mystery, showcasing Conan's exceptional detective skills.

3. Spy Next Door

The film challenges stereotypes by portraying Bob, a Chinese spy, as a hero who is skilled, caring, and capable. It promotes the idea that individuals should not be judged based on their cultural background or appearance but rather on their character and actions.
